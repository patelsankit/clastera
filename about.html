OUR HISTORY

Clastera International Tiles Exporting Company was founded in 2016 with the vision of becoming a leading player in the global tiles market. The company's journey began with a small team of passionate individuals who were committed to providing high-quality tiles to customers worldwide.

In the early years, Clastera International focused on establishing strong relationships with local tile manufacturers, ensuring a steady supply of diverse and innovative tile designs. With a focus on quality control and customer satisfaction, the company quickly gained a reputation for delivering superior products.

As the demand for tiles grew globally, Clastera International expanded its operations, establishing strategic partnerships with international distributors and retailers. This enabled the company to reach new markets and cater to the evolving needs of customers around the world.

Innovation has been a key driver of Clastera International's success. The company invested in research and development, constantly exploring new materials, designs, and production techniques to stay ahead of industry trends. This commitment to innovation allowed Cluster International to offer a wide range of tiles that met the changing preferences of customers and architects alike.

Over the years, Clastera International has built a strong distribution network, ensuring timely delivery of products to customers across continents. The company also focused on building long-term relationships with clients, providing exceptional customer service and support.

Today, Clastera International is recognized as a trusted name in the tiles industry, with a diverse portfolio of products that caters to both residential and commercial projects. The company continues to expand its reach and explore new markets, constantly striving to exceed customer expectations with its commitment to quality, innovation, and service.

As Clastera International looks to the future, it remains dedicated to its core values of integrity, reliability, and sustainability. The company aims to further strengthen its position as a global leader in the tiles market, while contributing to the beautification of spaces worldwide through its exceptional tile offerings.

OUR STRENGTHS

Our Vision

Our vision at Clastera International Tiles Exporting Company is to be the global leader in the tile industry, providing high-quality products that set new standards of excellence in design, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We strive to expand our reach and establish strong partnerships worldwide, ensuring that our diverse range of tiles inspires creativity and transforms spaces. With a commitment to sustainability and ethical practices, we aim to be a responsible corporate citizen, making a positive impact on the environment and communities we operate in. Above all, we aspire to be the go-to solution provider for all tile requirements, delivering exceptional customer service and support that exceeds expectations.

Our Mission

Our mission at Clastera International Tiles Exporting Company is to consistently deliver top-quality tiles to our global customers, meeting their unique needs and preferences. We strive to source the finest materials and employ cutting-edge manufacturing techniques to produce tiles that are durable, aesthetically pleasing, and environmentally friendly. With a focus on customer satisfaction, we aim to provide exceptional service, timely delivery, and competitive pricing. We are dedicated to fostering long-term partnerships with our clients, offering them a wide range of tile options and personalized solutions. Through our commitment to innovation, integrity, and sustainability, we aim to enhance the beauty and functionality of spaces worldwide.
